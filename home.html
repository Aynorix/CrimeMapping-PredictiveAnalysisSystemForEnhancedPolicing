Safety Tips

1. Stay Aware of Your Surroundings

Always be alert when walking in public places. Avoid distractions like mobile phones in unfamiliar areas.

2. Lock Your Doors and Windows

Ensure your home and vehicle are properly locked at all times to prevent unauthorized access.

3. Travel in Groups

Avoid walking alone at night. If possible, stay in well-lit areas and travel with friends or family.

4. Do Not Share Personal Information

Be cautious when sharing your location or personal details on social media or with strangers.

5. Learn Self-Defense

Basic self-defense skills can help you in emergencies. Consider taking a self-defense class.

6. Report Suspicious Activities

If you see something suspicious, report it to the authorities immediately.
